About Us

Murag Prep School is a division of Murag University focused on bringing quality education to parents who wish to raise their children as retirement plans. Our school offers advance courses in math, english, physical education and arts so that public school teachers can finally take a rest from incompetent children and continue to focus their stress on things that really do matter to them like trying to raise a family through a barely above minimum wage. We dream of a better Phillipines and the only way we can achieve that is through unity.

Recently, Murag Prep School has been granted as a Center of Excellence in Monster Education as well as a Level 7 accreditation by the Phillipine Accrediting Prep School Administration (PAPSA).

Structure of Classes

Murag Prep School believes that school should always be a place of solace for the child. It should foster curiosity, enhance creativity and most especially fun, that is why each class section here in our school is compose of a one three hour session, divided into six 30 minute chunks so that you can get the best of your tuition fee.

Admission Requirements

Enrollment is currently ongoing for the start of the school year.

  • The Age Qualification is as follows:
    A child must be four (4) years old for Kinder 1 and five (5) for Kinder 2, by August of the current school year.
  • Fill out the application form and upload requirements (NSO/PSA Birth Certificate and 2X2 Picture of the child).
  • Submit the hard copies within the school year 2022-2023:
    • Birth Certificate (Original Copy from NSO/PSA)
    • Recent passport size picture (1 copy)
