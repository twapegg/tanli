Hibalag Leaderboards

On August 24, I participated in a tournament at school called Hibalag Leaderboards. It was a competition featuring various video games, and I chose to compete in osu, a rhythm game. I had been practicing osu for a while and felt confident in my abilities.

When the day of the tournament arrived, I was nervous but excited to see how I would do. As it turned out, my hard work paid off and I ended up winning first place in the osu category. It was a great feeling to be recognized for my skills and to know that all of my practice had paid off. I received a certificate as well as a cash prize for being first place.

Overall, participating in the Hibalag Leaderboards tournament was a fantastic experience and I am grateful for the opportunity to compete and showcase my abilities.

Igniting Creativity thru Card Games

On August 22, I joined a Jackbox party session hosted by a teacher I had just met, Sir Te. At first, I wasn't sure what to expect, but I quickly found that the sessions were a lot of fun. Sir Te was a great host and had a great sense of humor, which really added to the enjoyment of the sessions.

It was also great to be able to engage with other people and have some fun while still in the comfort of my own home. As the sessions went on, I found myself looking forward to them more and more. The experience was chill yet excitingly fun as we answer trivia questions and see who the better guesser is.

Overall, joining the Jackbox party sessions was a great experience and I am grateful for the opportunity to connect with others and have a good time. It was also due to this that I was able to meet my favorite teacher, sir Te.

Dota 2 Tournament: "Rising Star"

I recently had the opportunity to watch a Dota 2 tournament hosted by a local gaming cyber cafe on November 20. The tournament was hosted by Groundzero and featured some of the best teams in the area.

As someone who loves video games, I was excited to see some high-level competition in person. Even though I haven't been able to play any games lately due to school, watching the different teams compete sparked something within me. It reminded me of how much I love gaming and how much fun it can be to watch skilled players in action.

The tournament was intense and there were some close matches, but in the end, one team came out on top. It was a great experience to see such talented players in action and it has inspired me to get back into gaming and continue to improve my own skills.

Pantawan Archery FunShoot

On November 19, I recently had the chance to watch an archery funshoot for tweens and teenagers. The event was held at Pantawan, a local archery range and featured a variety of skill levels. The participants competed for bronze, silver, and gold medals, which added an extra element of excitement to the competition.

As I watched the archers take their shots, I was amazed by their focus and determination. Even though some of the participants were beginners, they still gave it their all and tried their best to hit the target. It was inspiring to see how much they had improved over the course of the event and how much they enjoyed the sport.

Overall, it was a great experience to watch the archery funshoot and see the progress and passion of the young archers.
