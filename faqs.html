Frequently Asked Questions

Will my child/ren be safe throughout the class session?

Worry not about the safety of your child, as the Early Learning Academy security team is directly involved with the Avengers.

Where do your meals come from?

The school meals are served with fresh ingredients and prepared by a Michelin star chef, we assure you that your child will take a balanced diet.

What are your opening hours?

We open from 7:30 am until 6:00 pm. Our morning session runs from 7:30 am until 12:30 pm and our afternoon session runs from 1:00 pm until 6:00 pm.

Are there any free snacks?

Yes of course! Every obedient child will be rewarded with sweets, and a child will be given a star depending on their performance.

How do I pay for childcare?

You can pay for your childcare in many ways including cash, cheque, BACS, vouchers, standing order. We invoice you prior to the month for it to be able to be paid by the first of each month.
