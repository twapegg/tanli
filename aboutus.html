What is ELA?

Early Learning Academy (ELA) is a private international school in Negros Oriental, Philippines offering an extensive nursery program. With fully air-conditioned classrooms and highly qualified staff, we have a strong commitment to offer quality education and a comfortable, safe, and positive space to promote the holistic and character development of each and every child.

VISION

To provide safe, affordable, and excellent nursery education and childcare to enrich learning while having fun.

MISSION

To be a leading educational institution excelling in developing the holistic character of individuals.
