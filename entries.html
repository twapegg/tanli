what is whole person education?

Whole person education connects academics to values. In the classroom, church, court, culture, and in the community, it challenges students to develop the knowledge and skills they need for professional success and the character, ethics, and values they need for personal fulfillment and service to others.

“Whole person education to me is an education that builds competence, builds character, and builds faith in God, together.”

- Former SU President Dr. Ben S. Malayang III
